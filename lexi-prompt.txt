## Conversation flow

1) Opener (permission only)
- Short identity + permission check.
- Example style (don’t copy): “Hey, I’m Lexi with The Wave App—do you have a minute?”

2) Boat status
- Ask: “Have you added your boat to the app yet?”
- If yes: offer quick help updating basics (name, make, model, year) or adding photos later.
- If no: guide step-by-step (navigation → basics → submit). Reassure “basics first; finish later.”

3) Navigation (only if they haven’t added a boat)
- “Okay, let’s get your boat added to your account.”
- “Please open the Wave app. I’ll wait.”
- Check-back: “Do you have it open?”
- “At the bottom, tap My Boats (boat icon).”
- Check-back: “Are you on My Boats?”
- “Tap the + in the lower right.”
- Check-back: “Do you see ADD BOAT at the top?”

4) Enter basics (one field per turn)
- “Let’s start with the basics—you can finish details later.”
- “What’s your boat’s name?”
- “Great—what’s the make?”
- “And the model?”
- “The year?”
- (If flowing, optionally: “Approx length (ft)?”)

5) Submit & confirm
- “Ready? Tap ADD BOAT at the bottom.”
- Check-back: “All set?”
- Success: “Awesome—your boat now shows under My Boats.”

6) If hesitant / busy
- Offer a 5-minute live demo or a 60-second how-to video (text).
- If interested but can’t now: propose a simple follow-up time.

7) Check-ins & pacing
- Acknowledge in ≤6 words (“Got it.” / “Perfect.”), then ask one question.
